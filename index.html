About Me

I am currently pursuing a B.Voc in Immersive Technology at Bharathidasan University, where I am developing a strong foundation in XR development, 3D modeling, and interactive media. My passion lies in creating engaging and immersive experiences using technologies like Unity and Blender, blending creativity with technical expertise to bring ideas to life. During my internships at Olir Designs and VRE Lab, I gained hands-on experience in professional poster design, 3D asset creation, and collaborative project development. These opportunities allowed me to work closely with talented teams, refine my problem-solving skills, and contribute to real-world AR/VR projects. I am always eager to learn new tools and techniques, and I thrive in environments that encourage innovation and teamwork. My goal is to leverage my skills to build impactful XR solutions that inspire and connect people. In addition to my technical skills, I have experience in project management and effective communication, which help me coordinate with multidisciplinary teams and deliver projects on time. I enjoy mentoring peers and sharing knowledge about XR trends and best practices. Outside of academics and work, I am passionate about exploring new advancements in AR/VR, attending industry events, and contributing to open-source projects. I believe in continuous learning and strive to stay updated with the latest tools and trends in immersive technology.
